Toronto Island

Where or what is Toronto Island? Toronto Island one of the parks that both local (Torontoians) and tourists visit to enjoy the park. Toronto Island is famous especially during the summer. Currently, only selective people live in the Island. Originally, it was part of the land, not island.

Toronto Island is located south of downtown Toronto, preferably closer to the Harbourfront and Air Canada Centre. There are four harbours to the islands, one on north, one on west, one on east, one on south. Toronto Island is actually connected by pathway and bridges. It is about 5km long from West (Hanlan's Point) to East (Ward's Island). (1)

Just like mentions, inside the Island, there is always something to do. There's beaches, sport facilities, bike rentals, do canoe/kayak, picnics, and restaurants. It is really vibrant during month of May to September. However, during winter, most facilities remain closes and some area are so quiet. (1)

Also notice that there's city center airport connected. (1) For more information, take a look on this link, link 1, link 2, link 3.
