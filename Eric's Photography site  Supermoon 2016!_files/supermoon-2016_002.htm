'Supermoon' was closest full moon to Earth in almost 70 years | Toronto Star

MONTREAL-Canadians may want to turn their eyes to the night skies on Monday if they want to see the moon pass closer to the Earth than it has in almost 70 years. According to NASA, the so-called 'supermoon' will be the closest full moon to earth since 1948, and it won't be as close again until 2034.
