Christmas Market

Toronto Christmas Market

Soon or later, Christmas season is coming!
As a resident of Toronto, there is one Christmas event that I can think about.
The Christmas Market

It happened every years around the Month of November to December.This annual events bring most of the traditional and charm of the European Market inspired old and new. (2)
Here is some more description about the Christmas Market.

From toronto.com (1):
The Toronto Christmas Market takes over the Distillery Historic District for another year of holiday magic and romance. Now in its seventh year, the market has become one of the city's favourite holiday traditions, ranked one of the world's best Christmas markets by Fodor's Travel, USA Today, Mashable and more. Take in sparkling Christmas light canopies, traditional music and carols, dance performances and family-friendly activities like Santa's House, a ferris wheel, carousel and life-sized gingerbread house. Foodies can taste traditional European street-style food as well as Canadian holiday treats and sip on cold and warm brews in the beer gardens.


Youtube video:

It happen around the Distrillery District, it is located in east portion of Downtown Toronto. It has some niche cafes which I like, and shop surround by heritage building. The distrillery is located mix of both commerical and residental area.
Here is some description of Distrillery District and its location.

From Travel Advisor (3):
The Distillery District is an internationally acclaimed village of brick-lined streets and dozens of vibrantly restored Victorian Industrial buildings. It's one of Ontario's hottest tourist attractions and home to live theatres, galleries, fashion, design and jewelry boutiques, unique cafes and award-winning restaurants. It's all completely closed to traffic and just a few minutes walk from downtown Toronto.
