Santa Claus Parade

Few days ago, we had Santa Claus parade. It happened in last Sunday. And this year, it was the first day of the coldest day in November of 2016. For the record, it was over 10 degree during the day time before the parade. A lot of people show up including children to adult show up. Now, let's look at Santa Claus Parade.

Santa Claus Parade has been Toronto Tradition since 1905. (1) In that time most of the children and adult sits around the street just to see the Santa. The event includes animated floats and a lot of companies sponsor the marching bnads and a lot of participants wearing costume and a lot of people (mostly kids) cheered along the street. (1) This year, there is 25 differeent floats, 22 bands, 100 clowns, 1500 volunteers, and Santa. (2)

Believed or not, the Santa Claus Parade is 110 years old. Here is more detail about the history.
